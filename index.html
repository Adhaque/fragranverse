For first-timers

How it works

New to fragrance, or just new to Fragranverse? The whole thing in four steps. No jargon, promise.

1

Add what you own

Search each perfume and tap it. Wearing a clone? Search the name on your bottle and pick the closest match — clones absolutely count. Add up to 20.

2

Hit “Rate my collection”

We read each fragrance's notes, family, projection and occasion tags from a database of 5,500+ perfumes. Nothing is saved, no account needed.

3

Meet your scent persona

A 0–100 score, a grade, a persona that captures your shelf's character, and one honest tip to level up.

4

Get your next three bottles

We suggest three real perfumes — two upgrades in your favourite family, one to fill a gap in your wardrobe. Then download your badge and show it off.

What the score measures

Four things, 25 points each. Going deep on one family is a strength — specialists get celebrated, never scolded.

🌸

Diversity

How many of the 14 scent families you own — florals, woods, ouds, gourmands and more.

🏷️

Tier range

Budget, value, designer, niche. A range of price points tells its own story.

🗓️

Occasion coverage

Day, night, office, date, summer, winter — scents for every moment of your life.

💨

Sillage balance

Soft skin-scents through to room-filling beasts. The best wardrobes hold both.

Reading your grade

SMaster — balanced and deep. A complete wardrobe.
AExceptional — broad range, barely a gap in sight.
BSolid — or a focused Specialist who's gone all-in on one family.
CDeveloping — a strong start with the best part still ahead.
